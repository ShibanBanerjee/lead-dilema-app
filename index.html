Rules of the Game

The Goal

Grow to 25 accounts within 18 months without morale hitting zero or losing all your clients.

Key Metrics

  • Morale: If the team isn't overworked, morale recovers. Overworking them causes morale to drop. It also falls from financial losses and losing clients, but rises with profits and when you invest in the team.
  • Quality: Overworking your team hurts quality. Unhappy teams also produce worse work. If quality gets too low, you will start losing clients every month.

Your Actions

  • Accept Clients: Good for growth, but increases workload.
  • Focus on Quality: Sacrifices quantity for quality. You get a significant quality boost, but each team member can handle one less account.
  • Hire Staff: Increases your team's workload capacity, but costs money. New hires give a morale boost when they finish onboarding.
  • Buy/Build Tools: Increases team efficiency (capacity) and gives a morale boost, but has costs.
